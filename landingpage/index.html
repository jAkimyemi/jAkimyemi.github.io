How You Can Be Part Of Us

  1. The applicant must be a citizen of a country in Africa.
  2. The applicant must be a student in any educational institute in Africa.
  3. Applicant must be aged around 10 - 35 years old.
  4. Applicant must have completed primary school.
  5. Applicant must not be xenophobic or show any form of hate for cultural diversity.
  6. Applicant must be ready to commit and participate in all form of events and
    projects when chosen.
  7. Applicant must not use any of the community platforms for wrong motives,
    or illegal practices.
  8. All activities done must be academic driven or with an intentions to help others.
  9. Applicant must adhere to all instructions and abide by every rules stated here or
    later communicated to you by community leader or program directors.
  10. If applicant agrees to every rule and is eligible, applicants can proceed to apply
    @africancommunityofstudentsapplication.com.
  11. Application decision will be recieved after a month of review.
